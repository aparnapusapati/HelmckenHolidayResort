Important Information

💰

Room Taxes & Rates

Rooms: 15% tax applies. Rates based on double occupancy.

Children 11 years & under stay free when sharing a room with adults.

Single rollaway bed available for lower Log Rooms only.

🚐

RV & Tent Site Taxes & Rates

RV & Tent Sites: 5% tax applies. Rates based on 2 people.

Extra guests: $5.00 + tax per night.

Children (0–11 years) stay free.

🧾

Payment & Cancellation

A valid Visa or MasterCard is required at booking. Payment processed 14 days prior to arrival using the card on file.

Cancellations: Require 14 days’ notice prior to arrival.

Less than 14 days’ notice: Full charges will apply.

⏰

Check-In & Check-Out

Check-in: 3:00 PM | Check-out: 11:00 AM

Early check-in / late check-out may be available upon request (subject to availability).

🧹

Housekeeping & Cleanliness

Rooms: Cleaned daily; tea & coffee replenished by our housekeeping team.

RV Guests: Please maintain cleanliness and use designated waste and recycling areas.

🌙

Quiet Hours

Please respect quiet hours between 10:00 PM – 7:00 AM to ensure a peaceful stay for all guests.

🚭

Non-Smoking Policy

All rooms are non-smoking. Smoking is permitted only in designated outdoor areas around the property.

🐾

Pet Policy

Rooms: Only two designated rooms are pet-friendly.
Pet fee: $25/night per pet (max 2 pets per room, up to 75 lbs each).

Pets must be leashed or crated when outside rooms and not left unattended. Pets are not permitted in the restaurant, bar, or other indoor public areas.

Guests are responsible for any damage or noise disturbance caused by their pets.

RV & Tent Sites: Pets are welcome at no additional charge. Owners must keep pets leashed, clean up after them, and ensure quiet behavior.

Aggressive or disruptive pets may be asked to leave the premises. Service animals are welcome in accordance with Canadian accessibility standards.
